4. Advertising & Opt-Out

Our app displays ads. You can manage your ad preferences through your device settings (Limit Ad Tracking on iOS or Opt-out of Interest-Based Ads on Android).

5. Children's Privacy

Sudoku Smart Pro does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have accidentally collected such data, please contact us immediately.
