Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Sudoku Smart Pro mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We collect certain non-personal information automatically to improve our services and display relevant advertisements. This includes:

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and language.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Google Advertising ID (AAID) and Apple ID for Advertisers (IDFA).
  • Analytics: Game progress, feature usage, and technical performance data.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide high-quality services, we integrate with several third-party providers. Each has its own privacy policy regarding data handling:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying personalized and non-personalized advertisements.
  • Google Firebase: For app analytics, crash reporting, and cloud messaging.
  • AppLovin: For ad mediation and monetization services.
  • Supabase: For secure database management and user high scores.

4. Advertising & Choices

Our app uses AdMob and AppLovin to serve ads. You can opt-out of interest-based advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking.

5. Data Retention

Non-personal data (like high scores on Supabase) is stored as long as necessary to provide the service. Users can request data deletion by contacting us.
