Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the Sudoku Smart Pro mobile application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information we collect.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect personal information like names or emails. We only collect non-personal data to improve the app experience:

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and language.
  • Advertising IDs: Google Advertising ID (AAID) and Apple ID for Advertisers (IDFA).
  • Analytics: Usage data, crash reports, and game progress.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses trusted third-party providers to help us provide features and show advertisements. These services include:

  • Google AdMob: To serve personalized and non-personalized ads.
  • Google Firebase: For analytics and technical performance monitoring.
  • AppLovin: For ad mediation and monetization.
  • Supabase: For secure data storage and high scores.

4. Advertising & Opt-Out

We show ads to keep the app free. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings (Google Settings > Ads or iOS Privacy > Advertising).

5. International Laws (GDPR & CCPA)

We comply with global privacy standards:

  • GDPR (Europe): We process your data based on your consent. You can withdraw consent at any time via the app settings.
  • CCPA (California): We do not sell your personal information. You have the right to request access to the data we collect.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.
